Branding

Portfolio Overview

My portfolio website uses the most simple and intuitive black & white palette and layout with rich animation effects and interactions to provide users with a full interactive experience. I used a custom Bootstrap as the base framework, along with wow.js, Swiper, and animate.css to maximize the interactivity of the portfolio.

Potential Audience

The target audience for my portfolio is anyone who wishes to know about my work as a UI/UX designer, be it peers, companies, recruiters.
The primary audience will be any employer looking for an entry-level UI/UX designer.

Competitiveness

Compared with other portfolio websites, the biggest feature of my website is intuitive and rich interaction. The simple color palette allows viewers to browse through more detailed process analysis and reflection than others without any distractions.
