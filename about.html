Hi, I'm Yenan

I explore possibilities through design.

My interest in new media design led me to the School of Interactive Arts and Technology (SIAT). I enjoy learning about design methods and theories that help make people's daily lives easier. During the three years of study, I have improved my ability to conduct effective user research, propose reasonable solutions, make intuitive prototypes, and implement designs through code. Besides, I also had opportunities to practice video production as my hobby in several projects to explore different ways of expression. I am passionate about pushing boundaries to provide solutions for clients as a UI / UX Designer.
